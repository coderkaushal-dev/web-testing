Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Refund Policy

At Excess Ion Academy, we strive to provide the best coaching experience. Please read the following regarding refunds:

  • Course fees once paid are non-refundable.
  • In exceptional cases (e.g., technical failure from our side), a partial refund may be processed after management approval.
  • Refund requests must be submitted via email within 48 hours of payment.

2. Cancellation Policy

  • Enrollments cannot be cancelled once the student has gained access to the study material or attended a class.
  • The Academy reserves the right to cancel any batch/course due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases, a full refund or adjustment in another batch will be offered.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days and will be credited to the original payment method used during the transaction.
